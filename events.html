Weddings

You can book the restaurant or the whole hotel for all your guests. An exceptional menu, a beach for breathtaking photos or an unforgettable wedding ceremony, a spa for the day after.... Getting married at Medistone has many advantages. And yes, of course, the bride and groom sleep for free at the Medistone Hotel on the wedding day.

Celebrations

Our chef and his team will make sure that your dinner will be unforgettable (European or Dalmatian style). We have several Menu & BAR options for your celebration, so you can choose according to your budget and taste. If needed, we can help you organize the party: Decorations, entertainers, cakes and cupcakes, balloons, live music and so on.

Children's party

It's a great idea to have a children's party at Medistone Hotel. We can arrange two areas: for children and for adults. Of course we can help with decorations or entertainers. Needless to say, we have a children's menu and the kids say that they like our pizzas, burgers and chicken nuggets very much.
