Restaurant

Working hours: Monday – Sunday from 7.30 a.m. to 23.00 p.m.

We have our own stylish restaurant overlooking the sea. In the morning we serve breakfasts in a buffet version, for lunch we offer delicious meals and snacks. And for dinner you will be spoiled by our chef Ante. Ante fuses the local Dalmatian cuisine with international influences. Of course, fish is a must try here, and the catch of the day is something very special.

I answered the call of the kitchen, continuing a family tradition. My grandfather cooked for the former Yugoslav President Tito on his ship Galeb. My father has for many years delighted excursionists and hikers on Mount Kozijak. This combination of traditional cuisine and fine dining inspires me.
