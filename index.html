WHAT IS MEDISTONE?

MEDISTONE IS THE ONLY BOUTIQUE HOTEL ON THE BEACH IN MEDIĆI ON THE oMIŠ RIVIERA. A GREAT place FOR COUPLES AND FAMILIES WHO LOVE THE carefree atmosphere OF DALMATIAN PLACES, CRYSTAL CLEAR SEA AND PEBBLE BEACHES. Hotel Medistone is a place where you can spend hours lazing on the beach or taking a nap in your apartment overlooking the sea with the sound of the waves. If you are in the mood for delicious food, our hotel offers delicious, freshly prepared fish and meat dishes, and if you want to relax, choose the wellness area or the privacy of the pool or a Jacuzzi
